Fork me on GitHub Russian gas flows to Europe, storage rates
Russian gas flows to Europe, sourced from ENTSO-G, gas storage levels from GIE.

100GW = around 250 million m³/day (roughly). I don't know much about how the pricing works, but it appears to be over 2 €/m³.

Uzhgorod is on the Ukrainian border, and is also called Velke. The Yamal pipes are also called Mallnow, Jamal or Jagal. Nordstream meanwhile is also known as Greifswald in other tables.

As long as the EU storage withdrawal graph is positive, we are using up our reserves.

Data sourced daily from ENTSO-G, the European Network of Transmission System Operators for Gas and GIE, Gas Infrastructure Europe (thanks!). Please let me know urgently if I am missing any pipelines, of if I am misintepreting the data!
